What is BMI index?

The BMI (Body Mass Index) is a widely respected tool that allows us to assess our body weight. Its calculation is simple, requiring only numerical values for our height and weight. This index is obtained by dividing our body weight (expressed in kilograms) by the square of our height (expressed in meters). When the BMI result indicates a healthy body weight, we can be satisfied with the outcome! It is worth taking care to maintain such a proportion because monitoring it allows us to avoid many diseases.

While it may not be the most accurate method, it will give you an idea of whether you should lose or gain weight. To calculate your BMI, use the BMI calculator below and check if you are within a healthy weight range! However, remember that BMI is just one of many tools and may not provide a comprehensive picture of your health. Therefore, it is also advisable to consult with a doctor for a more thorough assessment of your weight and overall health.
